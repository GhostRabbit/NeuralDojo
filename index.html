Snake game?

  • AI learns to play snake using Genetic Algorithm and Deep learning
  • @github
  • What is different from examples above?
    • Brain is defined as 24 inputs, 16 hidden, 4 outputs
    • Inputs measure wall, food and self distance in 8 directions
    • Outputs are direction
    • Genetic training:
      • Each generation 2000 snakes
      • Snake starves after 200 moves
      • Eating prolongs life with 100 and snake grows)
      • Fitness is defined as lifetime^2 * 2 * length (for small snakes)
      • Natural selection occurs between generations
        • Best survives, rest is children
        • A child is made from two (random based on fitness) parents
        • A child brain is mutated based on parents
